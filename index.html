Why Choose Us

Unmatched Safari memories with Eagle Eye Expeditions.

Eagle Eye Expeditions is a wildlife passion, an endless love story of the Tanzania bush in a nutshell. Our Aim - is to make your safari memories unique, unforgettable and unmatched at the best quality possible. To do this, we design Safaris itineraries with goals to fulfill your African savanna wishes.

Reasons You should choose Eagle Eye Expeditions

The Bush Expeditions we provide are empowered by the well trained expert guides who make your safari a living dream. These are the most important part of your Safari and our guides are knowledgeable about the bush, and our country, and hospitable, and caring for your comfortable safari atmosphere whether during game drives or when handling you over to the overnighting bush destinations which we also select the best for our guests.

Reasons You should choose Eagle Eye Expeditions

  • We have amazing English-Speaking guides who will make your safari
  • All our clients arrive as guests and leave as friends that want to come back, they also advise their friends to go on a Safari with us
  • We have guided all sorts of guests from families, couples, groups, to photographers
  • We have comfortable cars perfectly designed to provide you with an Expedition to remember. They are also designed well for photographers
  • It is a licensed Tour Company with offices in Arusha Tanzania
