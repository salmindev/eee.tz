Low Season Safaris

Overview

These gapes are few, Beginning of May (end of rainy season) and November (end of dry Season), At this time of year, there is less traffic in the bush and for any guests interested in private Sightings in most places of the bush this is the perfect time and also the Accommodations are cheaper. Still you would see Big Cats, perhaps sometimes the Great Serengeti Migration but not during the main 2 highlights of the Migration.
