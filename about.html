About Us

Guides

Francis Thomas Kijazi (Chaba), is the Tour Guide that has the standards and qualities of making your Safari enjoyable and a memorable one. He was ones a Wildlife Teacher in the college for 4 years, then now a Guide for 17 years, the experience he has in the bush is unmatched! He knows the bush and understands its ecosystem as his home, he knows his country and appreciates cultures, he also loves showing guests wild animals and their interactions then telling facts and stories of the bush about them. What more do you need for your Safari than the guide whose biggest motivation comes from making guests smiling during the Safaris. Francis is also in to Birding, so for his lifelist is over 530 species and still very passionate about pointing birds out. He says, there's also much to tell about birds as well that's why he makes sure that, our guests get to know a few birds and their story before going back home.
